ABOUT US

Welcome to our winter wonderland in Park City, Utah! Immerse yourself in the thrill of skiing with our expert instructors and ski patrol guides. Hailing from the enchanting Patagonia, we bring our love for the mountains and a deep appreciation for Park City's unique culture to every lesson and backcountry tour. Whether you're a beginner seeking lessons or an adventurous spirit craving backcountry exploration, we've got you covered.

Additionally, experience the breathtaking backcountry on thrilling snowmobile tours led by our expert guides. Reserve your spot now for an unforgettable blend of Patagonian passion, education, and adventure in the heart of Park City's snowy landscapes!
